## Roles (Not Hierarchy)

### Members

**Definition**: Anyone contributing labor (ULU) or capital (bonds)

**Rights**:
- Vote on governance
- Earn profit distributions
- Access full financial ledger
- Propose changes
- Exit at any time

**Responsibilities**:
- Log hours honestly (ULU)
- Vote on proposals
- Maintain transparency
- Follow governance rules

---

### Active Contributors

**Definition**: Members logging ULU regularly

**Typical Contributions**:
- Bug bounty research (cybersecurity)
- Code development (Financial Coordinator)
- Documentation writing (operations)
- Customer support (future)

**Compensation**: ULU-weighted profit share

---

### Bond Holders

**Definition**: Members providing capital

**Typical Contributions**:
- $5,000-$50,000 in startup capital
- No active labor required
- Callable at any time

**Compensation**: 6% APR (paid daily)

---

### Hybrid Members

**Definition**: Both labor + capital contributors

**Example**:
- Alice: 40 hours/month ULU + $10,000 bond
- Income: Profit share (40 ULU) + $50/month bond interest

**Benefit**: Diversified income (guaranteed + variable)

## Why This Structure Works

### 1. **Eliminates Politics**

No promotions to fight over. No salary negotiations. No org chart battles.

**Result**: Focus on work, not politics

---

### 2. **Perfect Flexibility**

Want to work 10 hours this week? Fine, earn 10 ULU.
Want to work 60 hours next week? Fine, earn 60 ULU.

**Result**: Work-life balance + high performance

---

### 3. **Natural Meritocracy**

High performers self-select by working more hours.
Low performers self-select out (low earnings).

**Result**: No firing needed, natural attrition

---

### 4. **Infinite Scalability**

Add members without organizational complexity.
Each member = independent contributor.

**Result**: Linear growth, not exponential overhead
