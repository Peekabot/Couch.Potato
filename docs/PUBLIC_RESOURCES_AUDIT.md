## Additional High-Value Resources to Consider

### ðŸŽ“ Education & Training

#### **DOD SkillBridge Program**
- **What**: Civilian work experience during last 180 days of service (still on active duty)
- **Cost**: FREE (service members still receive full pay/benefits while interning)
- **Value**: Real-world job training with top employers (Amazon, Microsoft, Boeing, etc.)
- **Eligibility**: Active duty, 180 days or less remaining on contract
- **Why include**: Career acceleration, direct-to-hire pipeline, zero cost to veteran
- **Link**: https://dodskillbridge.usalearning.gov/

#### **Military Credentialing Programs (COOL)**
- **What**: Army, Navy, Air Force credentialing assistance programs
- **Cost**: FREE (covers exam fees for approved certifications)
- **Examples**: IT certs (CompTIA, Cisco), trade licenses (HVAC, welding), CDL
- **Eligibility**: Active duty and some transitioning veterans
- **Why include**: $200-$500+ in free certification exam vouchers
- **Links**:
  - Army COOL: https://www.cool.osd.mil/army/
  - Navy COOL: https://www.cool.osd.mil/usn/
  - Air Force COOL: https://www.cool.osd.mil/usaf/

#### **Apprenticeship.gov (DOL Registered Apprenticeships)**
- **What**: Paid on-the-job training + classroom instruction leading to certification
- **Cost**: FREE (apprentices are PAID while learning)
- **Fields**: Construction, IT, healthcare, advanced manufacturing, energy
- **Eligibility**: All veterans (many programs have veteran priority)
- **Why include**: Earn-while-you-learn model, industry-recognized credentials
- **Link**: https://www.apprenticeship.gov/veterans

#### **Google Career Certificates (Veteran Pathway)**
- **What**: 6-month certificate programs in high-demand fields
- **Cost**: $39/month via Coursera OR FREE via GI Bill through approved schools
- **Fields**: IT Support, Data Analytics, Project Management, UX Design, Cybersecurity
- **Eligibility**: All veterans (GI Bill eligible)
- **Why include**: Fast-track to tech careers, employer hiring network
- **Link**: https://grow.google/certificates/

#### **FEMA Training & Certifications**
- **What**: Free emergency management courses and incident command certifications
- **Cost**: FREE (all online, self-paced)
- **Certifications**: ICS-100, ICS-200, NIMS, Emergency Operations Center
- **Eligibility**: Open to all (veterans often prioritized for federal emergency jobs)
- **Why include**: Required for many government/contractor emergency management roles
- **Link**: https://training.fema.gov/is/

### ðŸ’¼ Business & Entrepreneurship

#### **SCORE Mentorship**
- **What**: Free one-on-one business mentoring from experienced entrepreneurs
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Services**: Business plan review, marketing strategy, financial planning
- **Eligibility**: All business owners (veteran-specific mentors available)
- **Why include**: Complements SBA training, personalized guidance vs generic courses
- **Link**: https://www.score.org/find-mentor

#### **Veterans Business Outreach Centers (VBOC)**
- **What**: Free training, counseling, and resource partner referrals
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Services**: Business plan development, access to capital, government contracting
- **Eligibility**: Veteran entrepreneurs
- **Why include**: More specialized than SBDC, veteran-specific expertise
- **Link**: https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/resource-partners/veterans-business-outreach-center-vboc-program

#### **Boots to Business (B2B)**
- **What**: 2-day entrepreneurship course + 8-week online course
- **Cost**: FREE
- **When**: Offered during TAP (Transition Assistance Program) or as "Boots to Business Reboot" post-service
- **Eligibility**: Service members, veterans, spouses
- **Why include**: Introduction to entrepreneurship fundamentals, connects to SBA ecosystem
- **Link**: https://sbavets.force.com/s/

### ðŸ—ï¸ Certifications & Licenses

#### **OSHA 10/30 Hour Certifications**
- **What**: Workplace safety training (10-hour for workers, 30-hour for supervisors)
- **Cost**: $30-$200 (low-cost, some veteran discounts available)
- **Industries**: Construction, general industry, manufacturing
- **Eligibility**: Open to all
- **Why include**: Often required for construction/manufacturing jobs and business insurance
- **Link**: https://www.osha.gov/training/outreach

#### **Real Estate License Prep**
- **What**: Training and exam prep for real estate agent license
- **Cost**: Varies by state (many states waive exam fees for veterans: $50-$300 savings)
- **Eligibility**: All (state-specific)
- **Why include**: Natural fit for veterans interested in property investment or side income
- **Note**: Some states offer free/discounted prep courses for veterans

#### **Commercial Driver's License (CDL)**
- **What**: Professional driving license for trucks, buses
- **Cost**: FREE via VA VR&E Chapter 31 or some state veteran programs
- **Demand**: High-demand career, median salary $48k-$75k
- **Eligibility**: Veterans (especially disabled veterans via VR&E)
- **Why include**: Fast career entry (4-8 weeks training), high demand, covered by VA benefits
- **Link**: https://www.va.gov/careers-employment/vocational-rehabilitation/

#### **FAA Certifications (Drone, Pilot)**
- **What**: Remote Pilot Certificate (Part 107 for commercial drones), Private Pilot
- **Cost**: Part 107 exam $175, flight training via GI Bill (worth $10k-$15k)
- **Eligibility**: All veterans (GI Bill eligible for flight training)
- **Why include**: Growing industry (drone surveying, inspections), aviation career pathway
- **Link**: https://www.faa.gov/uas/commercial_operators/become_a_drone_pilot

### ðŸ¥ Healthcare & Safety

#### **Red Cross Certifications**
- **What**: CPR, First Aid, AED, Lifeguarding, Babysitting
- **Cost**: $50-$150 (alternative to AHA, similar pricing)
- **Eligibility**: Open to all
- **Why include**: Alternative to AHA (some employers prefer Red Cross, others prefer AHA)
- **Link**: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class

#### **ServSafe Food Handler Certification**
- **What**: Food safety certification for restaurant/food service workers
- **Cost**: $15 (exam only) or $135 (course + exam)
- **Eligibility**: Open to all
- **Why include**: Required for many food service businesses, low cost, high ROI
- **Link**: https://www.servsafe.com/

### ðŸ›ï¸ Government & Federal Hiring

#### **Veterans' Preference for Federal Jobs**
- **What**: Hiring preference for veterans applying to federal government jobs
- **Types**: 5-point preference (all eligible veterans), 10-point preference (disabled veterans)
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Value**: Significantly increases chances of federal employment (competitive advantage)
- **Eligibility**: All honorably discharged veterans
- **Why include**: Federal jobs offer stability, benefits, and career growth
- **Link**: https://www.fedshirevets.gov/

#### **VEOA, VRA, Schedule A (Federal Hiring Authorities)**
- **What**: Special hiring authorities that allow veterans to be hired non-competitively
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Value**: Bypasses competitive application process for certain positions
- **Eligibility**: Varies (VRA: 2+ years active duty, disabled veterans for Schedule A)
- **Why include**: Faster path to federal employment
- **Link**: https://www.opm.gov/policy-data-oversight/veterans-services/vet-guide-for-hr-professionals/
