## PRESENTATION DELIVERY TIPS

### Before the Meeting:
- [ ] Send pilot proposal PDF 24-48 hours in advance
- [ ] Confirm attendees (who's in the room?)
- [ ] Prepare printed handouts (1-page pilot summary + sample intake form)
- [ ] Test screen sharing (if virtual)

### During the Meeting:
- [ ] Start with gratitude (thank them for time and mission)
- [ ] Stick to 20 minutes (leave 10 min for Q&A)
- [ ] Read the room (if they're engaged on slides 3-4, slow down; if glazed, speed up)
- [ ] Show enthusiasm (you believe in this, they should too)
- [ ] Ask for feedback (invite questions throughout, not just at end)

### After the Meeting:
- [ ] Send thank-you email within 24 hours (Email F template)
- [ ] Follow up with requested materials (attorney credentials, sample forms)
- [ ] Propose kickoff meeting date within 1 week
- [ ] Stay responsive (answer questions quickly)
